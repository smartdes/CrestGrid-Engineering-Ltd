1.4 Company Background

Crestgrid Engineering Limited is a Kenyan-based company engaging in General Technical Supplies, Engineering Services and Energy Solutions. Established by professionals in the power, renewable energy, safety and environment sectors, the Company leverages extensive expertise to deliver high-quality products and services to a diverse client base with a strong focus on innovation, safety and long term value. 
Our core competencies include power substation commissioning, electrical installations, solar photovoltaic systems, solar water heating, power backup systems, solar water pumping, security systems, energy audits, risk assessment, environmental impact assessment and audits. 
Through a client-centered approach and a commitment to engineering excellence, Crestgrid Engineering continues to drive impactful solutions that support development and environmental sustainability. 
Our company is fully registered as per Kenyan law, with the requisite professional certifications from National Construction Authority (NCA), Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority (EPRA) and  National Environment Management Authority (NEMA).
